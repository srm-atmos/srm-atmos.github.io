SRM-Met | Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Dataset Repository

SRM-Met utilizes a wide range of atmospheric, climate, lightning, satellite and numerical weather prediction datasets for research on weather and climate extremes, lightning science, climate variability, atmospheric electricity and artificial intelligence applications.
