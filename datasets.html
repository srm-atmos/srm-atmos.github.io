Datasets

SRM-ATMOS | Atmospheric Data Resources

Overview

SRM-ATMOS utilizes a wide range of observational, satellite, reanalysis, climate model and numerical weather prediction datasets for research on weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, satellite meteorology and artificial intelligence applications.

Reanalysis Datasets

ERA5 Reanalysis

Global atmospheric reanalysis dataset from ECMWF providing hourly meteorological variables at high spatial resolution.

NCEP-FNL Analysis

Global analysis data widely used for WRF model initialization and atmospheric diagnostics.

MERRA-2

NASA atmospheric reanalysis dataset for climate and aerosol studies.

Rainfall Datasets

IMD Gridded Rainfall

Daily gridded rainfall observations over India for climate and extreme rainfall studies.

GPM IMERG

High-resolution satellite precipitation estimates from the Global Precipitation Measurement mission.

TRMM

Tropical Rainfall Measuring Mission precipitation observations for historical rainfall analysis.

Satellite Datasets

INSAT-3D / INSAT-3DR

Indian geostationary satellite observations for cloud monitoring, convection tracking and weather analysis.

MODIS

Satellite observations of land, atmosphere, aerosols and cloud properties.

Sentinel Missions

Earth observation datasets supporting environmental and atmospheric studies.

Lightning Datasets

WWLLN

Worldwide Lightning Location Network data for lightning climatology and severe weather studies.

NRSC-LDSN

Lightning Detection Sensor Network observations for regional lightning monitoring over India.

Satellite Lightning Products

Lightning observations derived from satellite-based monitoring systems.

Climate Model Datasets

CMIP6

Global climate model simulations for climate change and future projection studies.

CORDEX

Regional climate model datasets for downscaled climate assessments.

IPCC Assessment Data

Climate projections and supporting datasets used for climate impact studies.

Numerical Model Datasets

WRF Simulations

High-resolution weather simulations generated using the WRF-ARW modelling framework.

WRF-ELEC Outputs

Lightning and atmospheric electricity simulations for thunderstorm studies.

Forecast Products

Research and experimental weather forecast datasets generated at SRM-ATMOS.

Laboratory Observation Datasets

Automatic Weather Station

Surface meteorological observations including temperature, humidity, pressure, rainfall and wind.

Aethalometer Observations

Black carbon aerosol measurements for air quality and climate research.

Sun Photometer Data

Atmospheric aerosol optical depth measurements.

LIDAR Measurements

Atmospheric boundary layer and aerosol profile observations.

Data Policy

SRM-ATMOS follows the data usage policies of the respective data providers. Users are encouraged to cite the original data sources when using publicly available datasets.

Research datasets generated within SRM-ATMOS may be made available for collaborative academic research upon request.
