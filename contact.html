Research Opportunities

Prospective Ph.D. scholars, project students, interns and collaborators interested in atmospheric sciences, weather forecasting, climate modelling, lightning science, satellite meteorology and artificial intelligence applications are encouraged to contact the faculty members.

Laboratory Facilities

The laboratory houses atmospheric observation systems including Lighting Sensor, Laser Disdrometer, Aethalometer, Sun Photometer and aerosol monitoring facilities. Researchers also have access to SRMIST High Performance Computing Centre (HPCC).
