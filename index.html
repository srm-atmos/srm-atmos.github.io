Welcome to SRM-Met

SRM-Met is a Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group under the Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory in the Department of Physics and Nanotechnology, SRM Institute of Science and Technology. The laboratory integrates atmospheric observations, satellite meteorology, lightning measurements, numerical weather prediction, climate modelling and artificial intelligence to investigate extreme weather and climate events.

Our research spans weather and climate extremes, thunderstorms, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, satellite meteorology, climate variability, numerical weather prediction and physics-informed artificial intelligence.
