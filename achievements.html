SRM-ATMOS | Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Overview

SRM-ATMOS has established a growing research ecosystem in atmospheric sciences through scientific publications, research scholar training, atmospheric observations, numerical modelling and interdisciplinary collaborations. The group actively contributes to research in weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, satellite meteorology, climate modelling and artificial intelligence applications.
