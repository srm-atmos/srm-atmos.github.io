Join SRM-ASRL

The Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory (ASRL) welcomes motivated students, researchers and collaborators interested in atmospheric sciences, weather and climate extremes, lightning science, numerical weather prediction, satellite meteorology and artificial intelligence applications.

PhD Opportunities

  • Weather and Climate Extremes
  • Lightning Science and Atmospheric Electricity
  • Numerical Weather Prediction (WRF)
  • Climate Modelling and CMIP6
  • Satellite Meteorology
  • Artificial Intelligence for Atmospheric Sciences

Student Internships

Undergraduate and postgraduate students are encouraged to undertake short-term research projects and internships in areas related to atmospheric sciences and climate studies.

Collaborations

ASRL welcomes collaborations with universities, research institutions, government agencies and industry partners working in weather, climate and environmental sciences.

How to Apply

Interested candidates may contact the faculty members of ASRL with their curriculum vitae, research interests and academic background.
