Research Opportunities

SRM-Met | Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Join SRM-Met

SRM-Met welcomes highly motivated students, researchers and collaborators interested in atmospheric sciences, weather and climate extremes, lightning science, numerical weather prediction, climate modelling, satellite meteorology and artificial intelligence applications.

We provide opportunities for doctoral research, student internships, collaborative research projects and skill development in modern atmospheric science tools and techniques.

Ph.D. Opportunities

Applications are invited from motivated candidates interested in pursuing doctoral research in atmospheric sciences.

Research Areas

  • Weather and Climate Extremes
  • Extreme Rainfall and Flood Forecasting
  • Lightning Science and Atmospheric Electricity
  • Numerical Weather Prediction (WRF)
  • Climate Modelling and Climate Change
  • Satellite Meteorology
  • Atmospheric Aerosols and Air Quality
  • Physics-Informed Artificial Intelligence
  • Atmospheric Boundary Layer Studies
  • Data Assimilation and Forecast Verification

Eligibility

  • M.Sc. Physics
  • M.Sc. Meteorology / Atmospheric Science
  • M.Tech. Atmospheric Sciences
  • M.Tech. Remote Sensing
  • M.Tech. Artificial Intelligence / Data Science
  • Equivalent postgraduate degree in a relevant discipline

Student Internships

B.Sc. / B.Tech. Internships

Short-term internships in atmospheric observations, weather analysis, climate data processing and scientific programming.

M.Sc. / M.Tech. Internships

Research-oriented internships involving numerical modelling, satellite meteorology and atmospheric data analysis.

Summer Training

Hands-on training in weather forecasting, climate analysis and atmospheric research tools.

Student Research Projects

Weather Forecasting Projects

Projects involving weather analysis, forecasting techniques and severe weather monitoring.

WRF Modelling Projects

Simulation of extreme weather events using the Weather Research and Forecasting (WRF) model.

Climate Data Analysis

Analysis of long-term climate datasets and climate variability.

Artificial Intelligence Projects

Applications of machine learning and physics-informed AI in atmospheric sciences.
