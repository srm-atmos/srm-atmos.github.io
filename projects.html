Research Projects

The Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory (ASRL) undertakes research projects focused on weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, numerical weather prediction, satellite meteorology and artificial intelligence applications in atmospheric sciences.

Ongoing Projects

Lightning Hazard Prediction over India

Development of lightning hazard prediction methodologies using observational datasets, numerical modelling and machine learning approaches.

Physics-Informed AI for Rainfall Forecasting

Integration of numerical weather prediction and artificial intelligence for high-resolution rainfall forecasting over Southern Peninsular India.

Atmospheric Electric Field Variability

Investigation of the influence of meteorological parameters and particulate matter on atmospheric electric field variability.

Earth-Atmosphere Radiation Budget

Studies of radiation budget components and their role in atmospheric processes and climate variability.
