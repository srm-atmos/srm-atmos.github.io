Research Projects

Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory

Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Project Portfolio

SRM-ATMOS undertakes research projects in weather and climate extremes, atmospheric electricity, lightning science, satellite meteorology, numerical weather prediction, climate modelling and artificial intelligence. The projects integrate atmospheric observations, remote sensing, modelling and data-driven techniques to address scientific and societal challenges.

Flagship Research Projects

Extreme Rainfall over Southern Peninsular India

Investigation of heavy rainfall events, flood-producing systems and atmospheric processes influencing extreme precipitation over Southern Peninsular India using observations and WRF simulations.

Integrated WRF and Physics-Informed AI Framework

Development of a hybrid framework combining numerical weather prediction and physics-informed artificial intelligence for improved understanding and forecasting of rainfall extremes.

Lightning Hazard Prediction over India

Development of lightning monitoring and prediction methodologies using lightning observations, satellite data and atmospheric diagnostics.

Atmospheric Electricity and Lightning Research

Thunderstorm Electrification

Investigation of electrical charging mechanisms and lightning production within convective storms.

Lightning Climatology

Analysis of spatial and temporal lightning distributions using WWLLN and regional lightning datasets.

Atmospheric Electric Field Studies

Research on atmospheric electricity, fair-weather electric fields and thunderstorm electrical environments.

Weather Forecasting and Modelling Projects

High-Resolution WRF Simulations

Convection-permitting simulations of thunderstorms, heavy rainfall events and severe weather systems over India.

Operational Forecast Development

Development of automated WRF-based weather forecasting systems for research and operational applications.

Data Assimilation Studies

Integration of observations and model simulations to improve weather prediction capabilities.

Climate Research Projects

Climate Variability and Change

Assessment of climate variability and long-term changes in temperature, rainfall and extreme events.

CMIP6 Model Evaluation

Evaluation of global climate model performance over India and South Asia.

Regional Climate Projections

Future projections of weather and climate extremes using climate modelling frameworks.

Satellite Meteorology Projects

INSAT Applications

Cloud monitoring, convection tracking and weather diagnostics using INSAT observations.

GPM Rainfall Validation

Evaluation of satellite rainfall estimates against observations and model outputs.

Remote Sensing of Atmospheric Processes

Use of satellite observations for studying clouds, precipitation and atmospheric dynamics.

Artificial Intelligence Applications

Physics-Informed Neural Networks

Development of physically consistent machine learning frameworks for atmospheric sciences.

Rainfall Forecasting

AI-assisted rainfall prediction using observational and model datasets.

Extreme Event Prediction

Application of artificial intelligence techniques to identify and predict weather extremes.
