About CLIQ 2025

The Climate Quiz Contest (CLIQ) is an initiative aimed at enhancing awareness of weather systems, climate variability, climate change, atmospheric hazards and environmental sustainability among students.

The programme encourages scientific thinking and promotes interest in atmospheric sciences, meteorology and climate research.
