CLIQ 2025 – Climate Quiz Contest

The Climate Quiz Contest (CLIQ 2025) was organized by the Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory (ASRL), Department of Physics and Nanotechnology, SRM Institute of Science and Technology, on the occasion of World Ozone Day observed on 16 September 2025. The programme was conducted to promote awareness of climate change, atmospheric sciences, ozone layer protection, weather hazards and environmental sustainability among students.

Outreach and Awareness Activities

ASRL regularly conducts outreach programmes, awareness campaigns, quizzes, seminars and scientific activities aimed at enhancing understanding of atmospheric sciences, weather and climate extremes, environmental sustainability and disaster preparedness among students and the public.
