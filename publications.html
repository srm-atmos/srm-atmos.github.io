SRM-ATMOS | Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Research Publications

SRM-ATMOS contributes to atmospheric science research through peer-reviewed journal publications and collaborative scientific research. Research activities focus on weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, satellite meteorology, numerical weather prediction, climate modelling and artificial intelligence applications.
