Research Publications

SRM-ATMOS contributes to atmospheric science research through publications in peer-reviewed journals, conference proceedings, book chapters and collaborative research outputs. Research activities focus on weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, satellite meteorology, numerical weather prediction, climate modelling and artificial intelligence applications.
