Collaborative Research

SRM-ATMOS actively promotes interdisciplinary and collaborative research in atmospheric sciences, weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, numerical weather prediction, satellite meteorology and artificial intelligence applications.

The research group welcomes collaborations with universities, research laboratories, government agencies, industries and international research organizations to address scientific and societal challenges related to weather, climate and environmental sustainability.

National Collaborations

India Meteorological Department (IMD)

Weather observations, forecasting and climate services.

Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology (IITM)

Monsoon research, climate variability and atmospheric modelling.

National Atmospheric Research Laboratory (NARL)

Atmospheric observations and boundary-layer research.

National Centre for Medium Range Weather Forecasting (NCMRWF)

Numerical weather prediction and data assimilation.

IIT Madras

Atmospheric science, climate research and environmental studies.

Indian Institute of Science (IISc)

Weather, climate and Earth system science research.

ISRO Telemetry, Tracking and Command Network (ISTRAC)

Rainfall Droplet Size Distribution studies using LASER Disdrometer Observations.

International Collaborations

Worldwide Lightning Location Network (WWLLN)

Global lightning observations and thunderstorm research.

NASA

Satellite observations and Earth system science.

NOAA

Climate monitoring and atmospheric datasets.

ECMWF

ERA5 reanalysis and weather forecasting products.

EUMETSAT

Satellite meteorology and climate applications.
